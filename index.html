Barbara J. Szabo

Introduction

I am an electrical designer with experience in a variety of electrical applications from commercial to utility/industrial. I also have some engineering experience and am gearing my career more towards the engineering aspect of electrical design in power generation and distribution. I am looking to stay within the utility and/or commercial industries and utilize my combined design/engineering experiences to explore new opportunities.

In line with my goal of transitioning towards more engineering in my career I've been pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on power systems and distribution. This type of focus falls in line with the experience I already have and the direction I want to go.

Work Experience

My work experience includes layouts of lighting, power, conduit, grounding, equipment and fire detection/notification systems. I utilize the NEC, NFPA, Life Safety Code, and numerous other building codes as well as company and client standards in creating the designs for the required systems.

Additionally, I've done power analysis, fault current calculations, and lighting level calculations. These are done in order to determine designs meet requirements and/or to determine what may be needed in order to complete a proper design.

Hobbies

I enjoy cross-stitch, crocheting, and knitting as relaxing activities during those rare moments I have to sit and relax. I like to find patterns or have patterns made from phots. The Detroit Lions blanket I made for my brother-in-law is an example of one of the crotchet blankets I've made.

Additionally, I love to read who-done-it type crime and mystery novels. One of my favorite authors is Patricia Cornwell. Her Kay Scarpetta series has had me intrigued for over 20 years. I also very much enjoy the Kinsey Millhone series (an alphabet based series starting with "A is for Alibi") by Sue Grafton. I've yet to complete either series but am in progress. Other authors I favor are Robin Cook, James Patterson, Tess Gerrisen, and Stephen King.

Something else I find enjoyment in, when I have the time, is baking and cooking. I do like trying new recipes and altering them until they work best for me. With baking I tend to gear more towards chocolate recipes but I have tried others. Some yummy items can be seen on the photos here. When I do cook I do it from scratch, no box mixed. I also like to make my own BBQ sauce. I do not use a recipe for that so it comes out a bit different every time, which is nice for less monotony.
