ABOUT

I am an electrical designer with experiance in a variety of electrical applications. From commercial to utility/industrial and including lighting, equipment, grounding, fire alarm, and more. I also have some engineering experiance and am gearing my career more towards the engineering aspect of electrical design. I am looking to stay with in the utility and/or commercial industries and utilize my combined design/engineering experiences to explore new opportunites.

In line with my goal of transitioning towars more engineering in my career I've been pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on power systems and distribution. This type of focus falls in line with the experience I already have and the direction I want to go.

Work Experience

Lighting

I've done lighting design at a variety of types of establishments. This included normal lighting, emergency lighting, task lighting, and decorative lighting. I enjoy lighting design very much. In commercial applications there is a creative aspect to lighting. Beyond layout, there is a measure of enjoyment in choosing fixtures that compliment the architectureal theme of an area. Not to mention choosing colors to compliment or even to contrast, with the surroundings.
